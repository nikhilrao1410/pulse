About Us

Welcome to Cardio Companion, a platform created with the vision of bringing comprehensive health services to your fingertips. Developed by a passionate team of B.Tech students from KIIT, our project aims to address the growing need for accessible health-related information and tools that can empower individuals to take charge of their well-being.

Our Mission

In a world where health information is vast but not always accessible or understandable, we strive to break down these barriers by providing clear, reliable, and actionable health insights. Whether it's through the latest health news, tracking your BMI and understanding its implications, monitoring your pulse rate using just a webcam, or recommending exercises tailored to your health metrics, we're here to guide you towards a healthier lifestyle.

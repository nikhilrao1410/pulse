What is Pulse Beat?

Pulse Beat is a heart rate monitoring system that uses computer vision techniques to analyze the subtle changes in facial skin color caused by blood flow.

How does Pulse Beat work?

Pulse Beat works by capturing video from your webcam and analyzing the color changes in your face to detect your heart rate.

How can I get support for Pulse Beat?

For any questions or technical support related to Pulse Beat, you can contact our customer support team through the website or email.

How often should I use Pulse Beat?

Pulse Beat can be used as often as desired. It's particularly useful for monitoring your heart rate during physical activity or periods of relaxation.

Can Pulse Beat detect irregular heart rhythms?

Pulse Beat is primarily designed for measuring heart rate and may not detect irregular rhythms. For detecting irregularities, it's recommended to consult a healthcare professional.

Is my data secure with Pulse Beat?

Yes, Pulse Beat prioritizes user privacy and data security. Your heart rate data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to any external servers.

Can Pulse Beat be used for medical diagnosis?

Pulse Beat is intended for general wellness purposes only and should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
